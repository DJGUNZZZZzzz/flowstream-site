PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS: This Privacy Policy explains how FlowStream collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you directly provide when you:

  • Create an account: Username, email address, password, date of birth
  • Complete your profile: Display name, bio, profile picture, social media links
  • Make purchases: Payment information (processed by third-party providers)
  • Communicate with us: Support requests, feedback, correspondence
  • Create content: Streams, chat messages, comments, panels

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use FlowStream, we automatically collect:

  • Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks, interactions
  • Stream data: Viewing history, watch time, streams followed
  • Performance data: Load times, errors, technical diagnostics
  • Location data: General location based on IP address

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you logged in
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

See our Cookie Policy for more details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

2.1 Provide and Improve Services

  • Operate and maintain the Platform
  • Process transactions and subscriptions
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize your experience
  • Develop new features and improvements
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends

2.2 Communications

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Notify you of platform updates
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send security alerts

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect against abuse and spam
  • Investigate violations
  • Comply with legal obligations

2.4 Advertising and Analytics

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Analyze platform metrics
  • Understand user behavior

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

3.1 Other Users

Your public profile information, streams, and chat messages are visible to other users. You control what information is public through your privacy settings.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who help us operate the Platform:

  • Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics)
  • Email service providers
  • Customer support tools

3.3 Business Transfers

If FlowStream is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Respond to government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

4. Your Privacy Rights

4.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

4.2 Correction and Deletion

You can:

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct inaccurate information

4.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Adjusting your notification settings
  • Contacting support@flowstream.io

4.4 Cookie Controls

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings
  • Our cookie consent tool
  • Third-party opt-out tools

4.5 Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals, but you can control tracking through cookie settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services
  • Required by law
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

FlowStream is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Users aged 13-17 require parental consent to use the Platform.

8. International Data Transfers

FlowStream is based in the United States. If you access the Platform from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other countries.

We comply with applicable data protection laws regarding international transfers.

9. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@flowstream.io

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@flowstream.io

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
