COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

BUILDING A POSITIVE COMMUNITY: FlowStream is committed to creating a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment for all users. These guidelines help us maintain a positive community.

1. Our Values

FlowStream is built on these core values:

  • Respect: Treat everyone with dignity and kindness
  • Inclusivity: Welcome people from all backgrounds
  • Safety: Protect users from harm and harassment
  • Authenticity: Be genuine and honest
  • Creativity: Encourage original and innovative content

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

2.1 Harassment and Bullying

We do NOT tolerate:

  • Targeted harassment or bullying of individuals or groups
  • Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)
  • Stalking or unwanted contact
  • Brigading or coordinated harassment
  • Encouraging others to harass someone

2.2 Hate Speech and Discrimination

Prohibited content includes:

  • Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Slurs and derogatory language
  • Symbols or imagery associated with hate groups
  • Content that promotes discrimination or violence
  • Denial of well-documented violent events

2.3 Violence and Threats

We prohibit:

  • Threats of violence against individuals or groups
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Content that glorifies or encourages violence
  • Instructions for making weapons or explosives
  • Content depicting animal cruelty

2.4 Sexual Content

Not allowed:

  • Sexually explicit content or pornography
  • Sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance)
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery
  • Sexual solicitation or prostitution
  • Overly sexualized content or behavior

2.5 Self-Harm and Suicide

Prohibited:

  • Content that promotes or glorifies self-harm
  • Suicide instructions or encouragement
  • Graphic depictions of self-harm

Note: We allow discussions about mental health and recovery. If you or someone you know needs help, please contact a crisis helpline.

2.6 Illegal Activities

We prohibit content that:

  • Promotes or facilitates illegal activities
  • Depicts illegal drug use or sales
  • Shows illegal gambling
  • Involves fraud or scams
  • Violates intellectual property rights

2.7 Spam and Manipulation

Not allowed:

  • Spam, including repetitive messages or content
  • View botting or artificial engagement
  • Misleading links or phishing attempts
  • Impersonation of others
  • Manipulation of platform features

2.8 Misinformation

We prohibit:

  • Deliberate spread of false information
  • Health misinformation that could cause harm
  • Election misinformation
  • Manipulated media (deepfakes) without disclosure

3. Streaming Guidelines

3.1 Content Standards

Streamers must:

  • Comply with all platform rules
  • Accurately categorize their content
  • Use appropriate titles and thumbnails
  • Moderate their chat effectively
  • Respect copyright and licensing

3.2 Prohibited Stream Content

  • Streams primarily focused on prohibited content
  • Unmoderated toxic chat environments
  • Dangerous stunts or challenges
  • Streaming from private spaces without consent
  • Streams designed to evade bans

3.3 Mature Content

Mature content must be:

  • Clearly labeled as mature
  • Age-gated appropriately
  • Within acceptable limits (no explicit sexual content)
  • Contextually appropriate

4. Chat and Interaction Guidelines

4.1 Respectful Communication

In chat, you should:

  • Be respectful and constructive
  • Avoid excessive caps lock or spam
  • Respect moderator decisions
  • Stay on topic
  • Welcome new community members

4.2 Prohibited Chat Behavior

  • Harassment or personal attacks
  • Spam or excessive emote use
  • Backseat gaming (unless welcomed)
  • Spoilers without warnings
  • Promoting other channels without permission

5. Creator Responsibilities

5.1 Moderation

Creators must:

  • Actively moderate their communities
  • Appoint responsible moderators
  • Set clear chat rules
  • Address violations promptly
  • Report serious violations to FlowStream

5.2 Disclosure

Creators must disclose:

  • Sponsored content or paid promotions
  • Affiliate links
  • Financial relationships with brands
  • Giveaway terms and conditions

5.3 Authenticity

Creators should:

  • Be genuine and honest with their audience
  • Not misrepresent their identity or credentials
  • Avoid deceptive practices
  • Give credit to others' work

6. Enforcement

6.1 Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the report button on the content
  • Provide specific details about the violation
  • Include timestamps for video content
  • Email serious violations to: report@flowstream.io

6.2 Our Review Process

When we receive a report:

  • We review it within 24-48 hours
  • We assess the context and severity
  • We may request additional information
  • We notify you of the outcome

6.3 Consequences

Violations may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Violating content is deleted
  • Temporary Suspension: 1-30 days depending on severity
  • Permanent Ban: Severe or repeated violations
  • Legal Action: For illegal content or serious harm

6.4 Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was incorrect:

  • Submit an appeal within 30 days
  • Explain why you believe the decision was wrong
  • Provide any relevant evidence
  • Email: appeals@flowstream.io

7. Special Protections

7.1 Minors

We provide extra protection for users under 18:

  • Stricter content moderation
  • Limited data collection
  • Parental controls available
  • Zero tolerance for exploitation

7.2 Vulnerable Groups

We take extra care to protect:

  • Victims of harassment or abuse
  • Marginalized communities
  • People in crisis
  • Those targeted by hate groups

8. Positive Community Practices

8.1 Encouraged Behavior

We encourage:

  • Supporting fellow creators
  • Constructive feedback
  • Celebrating diversity
  • Sharing knowledge and skills
  • Collaborating with others
  • Reporting violations

8.2 Community Programs

FlowStream offers:

  • Creator mentorship programs
  • Community events and challenges
  • Recognition for positive contributors
  • Resources for mental health and safety

9. Platform Integrity

9.1 Account Security

Protect your account by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Enabling two-factor authentication
  • Not sharing your credentials
  • Reporting suspicious activity

9.2 Fair Use

Users must:

  • Not create multiple accounts to evade bans
  • Not buy or sell accounts
  • Not use automation or bots
  • Not exploit bugs or vulnerabilities

10. Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated as our community grows and evolves. We will:

  • Notify users of significant changes
  • Provide reasonable notice before enforcement
  • Consider community feedback

11.2 FlowStream Support

Contact Us:

Safety Team: safety@flowstream.io

REMEMBER: These guidelines exist to keep FlowStream safe and welcoming for everyone. By using the platform, you agree to follow these rules. Thank you for being part of our community!
